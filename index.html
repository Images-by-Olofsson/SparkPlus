Features

Real-Time Notifications

Receive instant browser notifications when tickets are assigned or updated in Spark.

Ticket Overview

View all your assigned tickets at a glance directly from the browser toolbar.

Quick Actions

Perform common ticket operations directly from the extension popup without switching context.

Configurable

Customise notification filters, refresh intervals, and display preferences to suit your workflow.

Installation

1

Download

Click the download button above to get the latest addon.zip file.

2

Extract

Unzip the downloaded file to a permanent location on your computer.

3

Enable Developer Mode

Open chrome://extensions in your browser and enable Developer mode in the top-right corner.

4

Load Extension

Click Load unpacked and select the extracted folder. SparkPlus will now appear in your toolbar.
